# Dark Mode QA Checklist

## Screens To Verify
- Garage
- Vehicle Overview
- Vehicle Info
- Maintenance
- Reminders
- Parts
- Add Vehicle form
- Service form
- Reminder form
- Part replacement form
- Receipt photo full-screen viewer
- Backup and Advanced Settings screens

## Contrast Checks
- Primary text stays readable on every list row, card, and form section.
- Secondary text stays readable and does not fade too far into the background.
- Buttons, capsules, and badges still look tappable in both appearances.
- Card surfaces remain visually separate from the surrounding background.
- Placeholder icons and vehicle image fallbacks remain easy to see.

## Reminder Urgency Checks
- Overdue reminders are clearly stronger than due-soon reminders.
- Due-soon reminders remain easy to notice in both Light and Dark Mode.
- Completed reminders feel de-emphasized without becoming hard to read.
- Urgency badges keep enough contrast against their capsule backgrounds.
- The overview status strip still communicates urgency at a glance.

## Sheet And Modal Checks
- Add/edit sheets present with readable forms in both appearances.
- Alerts remain readable and calm in both appearances.
- The receipt photo viewer still feels intentional with its dark presentation.
- Share sheets and import/export sheets present cleanly from the app.

## Image Placeholder Checks
- Vehicle placeholders look balanced and not overly bright in Light Mode.
- Vehicle placeholders do not glow or flatten awkwardly in Dark Mode.
- Empty-state icons remain visible and not washed out.
- Receipt placeholder content is readable over the dark viewer background.

## Empty State Checks
- Empty garage message remains clear in both appearances.
- Empty maintenance, reminders, and parts states keep one obvious next action.
- Empty-state helper text keeps enough contrast in Dark Mode.

## Light Vs Dark Comparison
- Garage feels calm and trustworthy in both appearances.
- Vehicle Overview still feels like the payoff screen in both appearances.
- Reminder urgency remains obvious without feeling harsh.
- Backup and Advanced Settings still read clearly and feel polished.
- No row, card, or badge disappears into the background.
- No hardcoded surface looks out of place when switching appearance.
