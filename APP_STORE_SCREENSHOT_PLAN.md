- Keep each screenshot bright, readable, and focused on one main idea.

## Notes For Better Conversion
- Use one main demo vehicle across most screenshots for continuity.
- Avoid showing Advanced Settings in App Store screenshots.
- Avoid empty states in screenshots unless you want a separate marketing image later.
- Make sure the reminder urgency screenshot is visually obvious because it quickly communicates ongoing value.
